## Jaisel Singh

Jaisel Singh maintains RoBlog as a home for robotics notes, engineering writeups, research digests, and the occasional longer reflection that needs more room than a README. The site is meant to stay lightweight: Markdown first, durable links, and posts that can grow from rough notes into useful references.

## Ben Evans

[Ben Evans](https://github.com/bennevans) is invited as a co-author for shared RoBlog posts. Ben's role here is collaborative: adding another technical eye, helping shape posts that benefit from dialogue, and co-authoring pieces where the best version comes from two people thinking through the problem together.

## Collaboration

When a post is written together, the front matter should list both authors:

```yaml
author:
  - Jaisel Singh
  - Ben Evans
```

That keeps authorship visible on the post page, in feeds, and in the generated site metadata.
